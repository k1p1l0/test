## Deeplink join:

Doesn’t work with group chats, platform limitation

For example you have a link to a bot on a web-site and in facebook group and you want to split that traffic. So you make a t.me/botlink?source=web and t.me/botlink?source=facebook

/start
Hi, Jeremy! It’s always nice to see people coming from Facebook
